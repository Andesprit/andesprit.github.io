# Impeccable Project Context

## Design Context

### Users
Two audiences: (1) business owners and teams (small to large) evaluating Andesprit for custom chatbots, LLM agents, automations, or webpages; (2) developers discovering the open-source tools. Both arrive from Google or the GitHub org and need to grasp the offering in under a minute.

### Brand Personality
Grounded, precise, high-altitude. A serious engineering studio from the Andes: craftsmanship and intelligence, not startup hype. Tagline heritage: "The best minds on the Andes" / "Smart tools to do any Job".

### Aesthetic Direction
Light, warm stone paper with terracotta primary accent and high-altitude sky blue as a restrained secondary (open source, links). Dark "dusk" band for contact. Andean motif: ridge-line / stepped terrace mark. Editorial index-style product listing, asymmetric layouts, no card grids. Typography from Andean foundries: Archivo Expanded (Omnibus-Type, Buenos Aires) for display, Alegreya Sans (Huerta Tipografica) for body — both self-hosted.

### Design Principles
1. Parse-ability first: a visitor scans products, open source, and services without reading a paragraph.
2. One terracotta accent doing real work (CTAs); sky blue only marks open source and links.
3. Editorial index rows over icon-card grids.
4. Zero external requests; near-100 Lighthouse; semantic HTML for SEO.
5. The Andes story is real (type, motif, copy) — never kitsch.
