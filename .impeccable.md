# Impeccable Project Context

## Design Context

### Users
Two audiences: (1) business owners and teams (small to large) evaluating Andesprit for custom chatbots, LLM agents, automations, or webpages; (2) developers discovering the open-source tools. Both arrive from Google or the GitHub org and need to grasp the offering in under a minute. Bilingual: English at /, Spanish at /es/ (auto-detected, manual toggle in nav).

### Brand Personality
Grounded, precise, high-altitude. A premium independent software studio rooted in Colombia's green Andean cordilleras: craftsmanship and intelligence, not startup hype.

### Aesthetic Direction
Deep green-navy (#071f18) with leaf-green accents (#347a46) and moss highlights on a pale sage paper (#f1f3e9). Editorial hero with original Andean imagery (assets/andesprit-hero-colombia.jpg), product grid cards, dark open-source band, photo-anchored services section, dark contact band with paper form card. Triangular peak mark (favicon + assets/andesprit-mark.svg). Typography: Archivo Expanded (display) + Alegreya Sans (body), self-hosted; shared styles.css.

### Design Principles
1. Parse-ability first: a visitor scans products, open source, and services without reading a paragraph.
2. Green Andean identity carried by imagery and color, never kitsch.
3. Original editorial imagery only; no stock photos, no text baked into images.
4. Zero external requests; near-100 Lighthouse; semantic HTML for SEO; hreflang for EN/ES.
5. Both languages stay in lockstep: any copy or structure change lands in index.html and es/index.html together.
