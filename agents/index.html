Five useful patterns

Where agents create leverage.

These are starting points, not fixed packages. We shape the interface, integrations and level of autonomy around the way your business already works.

  1. 01 · Knowledge

    Business chatbots that know your information.

    Give customers or teams a clear conversational way to find frequent answers and understand policies, manuals, contracts or product documentation.

    • Answers grounded in approved sources
    • Citations back to relevant documents
    • Escalation when confidence or permissions require it

  2. 02 · Commerce

    Shopping agents that guide the whole decision.

    Help shoppers describe what they need, compare the right products, add items to cart and move confidently toward checkout.

    • Natural-language product discovery
    • Inventory-aware comparisons and recommendations
    • Cart and checkout actions through existing commerce tools

  3. 03 · Scheduling

    Appointment agents that keep calendars moving.

    Let people book, reschedule and confirm appointments conversationally—ideal for salons, healthcare practices, consultants and other appointment-based businesses.

    • Availability from the real calendar
    • Service, staff and duration rules
    • Reminders, changes and cancellation handling

  4. 04 · Documents

    Document flows that become structured data.

    Process high volumes of forms, invoices, reports or records and turn the important fields into clean, reviewable information.

    • Classification, extraction and normalization
    • Validation rules and exception queues
    • Structured output for databases or downstream systems

  5. 05 · Integration

    Automations that keep services in sync.

    Connect the systems that hold customer, operational and commercial information so updates flow once, reliably, to every place that needs them.

    • Email, CRM, calendar and inventory coordination
    • Event-driven updates and approval steps
    • Shared context for reporting and follow-up
